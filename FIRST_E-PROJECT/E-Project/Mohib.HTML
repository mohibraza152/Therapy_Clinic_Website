About

Hello! I’m Mohib Raza, Mohib is an experienced relationship and psychosexual therapist, who works with couples and individuals. She will offer you a safe and supportive space to work either face to face or online. Mohib has worked for many years with the Macmillan Cancer organisation and can offer support to those facing life changing diagnosis, loss and grief. She also works with anxiety and stress related issues using other therapeutic models including cognitive behavioural therapy (CBT).

Mohib is a registered member of BACP and COSRT ( College of Sexual and Relationship Therapists.) She has also worked for many years for the Relate organisation and is Relate trained.

I respond to a broad spectrum of issues including depression, anxiety, unwanted sexual behaviour, relationship struggles, anger, trauma, burnout, unresolved family issues and more severe mental illnesses. In addition, I provide couples counselling, professional coaching and debriefing services.
